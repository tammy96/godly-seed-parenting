ABOUT US

Godly Seeed Parenting Outreach is a non-governmmental whoose main aim is to create awareness for parents and to-be parents and to equip them with resources that will empoweer them to raise Godly Seeds unto the Lord as enshrinned in Malachi 2:15b.

PROGRAMS & RESOURCES

Enlightenment

This is our way of educating parents and parents to-be on the need to raise Godly children unto God.

Tetiary Institution Outreachs

This is our way of reaching out to students in college/university with the need to prepare for Godly parenting.

Community Outreach

We reach communites with thr message of Godly parenting. We reach out to them with whatever we can to elevate the suffering of parents and children alike.

Parenting Education

We run a parenting school and fellowship to equip parents with relevant information that will make their parennting assignment easier and more effective.

Volunteer Training

This is a training program to equip volunnteers with the required knowledge needed to reach out to parents.

Conference Speaking

We are into conference speaking, school enlightenment etc.
